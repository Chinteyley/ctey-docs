**Last updated:** November 13, 2025

SnapPosts respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how your data is protected.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Photos you upload:** Used only to generate posters. They are not shared or stored permanently.
- **App usage data:** Basic analytics (such as device type or app version) to improve performance.
- **No personal account data:** The app does not require login or collect personal identification unless you contact us directly.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To generate posters using AI.
- To improve app quality, fix bugs, and understand general usage patterns.
- To provide customer support when you reach out.

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored
- Uploaded photos are processed securely and removed after the poster is generated.
- SnapPosts does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app may use trusted third-party tools (such as analytics or AI models). These services follow their own privacy rules and handle data safely.

## 5. Children's Privacy
SnapPosts is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 6. Your Rights
You can request deletion of any information you shared with us by contacting our support email.

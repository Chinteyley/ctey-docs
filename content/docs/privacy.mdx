**Last updated:** February 2026

This privacy policy describes how ctey.dev ("the Site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit. This Site is a personal documentation and portfolio website operated by Chintey Ley.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Data

When you visit the Site, certain information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies:

- **Server logs**: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and pages visited may be recorded in server logs for security and performance monitoring.
- **Analytics**: The Site may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand traffic patterns, popular pages, and visitor demographics. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify individual visitors.

### Information You Provide

The Site does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. If you contact me through email or social media links on the Site, any information you share is handled according to the respective platform's privacy policy.

## Cookies

The Site may use essential cookies for basic functionality such as theme preferences (light/dark mode). No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## Third-Party Services

The Site is hosted on Vercel and uses Cloudflare for DNS and security. These services may collect standard web traffic data according to their own privacy policies:

- [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

## SnapPosts App

If you are looking for the privacy policy for the SnapPosts mobile application, please visit the [SnapPosts Privacy Policy](/docs/projects/snappost-privacy) page.

## Data Retention

Server logs and analytics data are retained for a reasonable period for security monitoring and performance analysis, after which they are deleted.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request information about what data is collected
- Request deletion of any personal data
- Opt out of non-essential cookies through your browser settings

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent changes were made.
